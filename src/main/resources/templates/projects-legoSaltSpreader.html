Lego Salt Spreader

Material: Lego Mindstorms ® NXT kit for the construction of the robot.

Language used: RobotC (C++ based)

Purposes: to spread salt on side walks and other slippery surfaces.

This vehicle turns in the opposite direction if it touches an obstacle.

When an obstacle is in between the path and the other side, this vehicle can also cover the missing side.

After it finishes, it will display the amount of distance covered and the amount of salt used.
